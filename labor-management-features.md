# Labor Management Features

## Feature: Time Tracking System
**Description:** Comprehensive employee time tracking and labor cost management.

**Requirements:**
- Employee clock-in/out functionality
- Project time allocation
- Activity-based time tracking
- Overtime calculation and approval
- Time-off request and approval system
- Attendance monitoring
- Mobile time tracking app
- Integration with payroll system

**Technical Requirements:**
- Biometric/facial recognition integration
- GPS location tracking for field work
- Mobile app development (React Native)
- Integration with HR systems
- Automated payroll calculations
- Compliance with labor laws

**Estimated Development Time:** 100 hours
**Priority:** High

## Feature: Labor Cost Analysis
**Description:** Detailed analysis of labor costs and productivity metrics.

**Requirements:**
- Labor cost per project/OT
- Productivity metrics by employee/department
- Labor efficiency analysis
- Cost variance analysis (budgeted vs. actual)
- Labor cost forecasting
- Skill-based costing
- Training cost tracking
- Labor utilization reports

**Technical Requirements:**
- Advanced cost allocation algorithms
- Productivity measurement system
- Forecasting models
- Integration with accounting
- Custom reporting engine
- Data visualization tools

**Estimated Development Time:** 70 hours
**Priority:** High
