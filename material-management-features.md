# Material Management Features

## Feature: Material Requisition System
**Description:** Complete material planning and procurement workflow.

**Requirements:**
- Material requirement planning based on work orders
- Supplier selection and quote comparison
- Purchase order generation
- Material tracking from order to delivery
- Inventory management integration
- Material cost tracking and analysis
- Supplier performance monitoring
- Automated reorder point alerts

**Technical Requirements:**
- MRP (Material Requirements Planning) engine
- Integration with supplier systems
- Barcode/QR code tracking
- Inventory optimization algorithms
- Supplier rating system
- Automated procurement workflows

**Estimated Development Time:** 90 hours
**Priority:** High

## Feature: Supplier Management Portal
**Description:** Comprehensive supplier database and performance tracking.

**Requirements:**
- Supplier information management (contact, capabilities, certifications)
- Supplier performance metrics (on-time delivery, quality, cost)
- Supplier classification and approval process
- Supplier communication tracking
- Contract management
- Supplier audit scheduling
- Preferred supplier program

**Technical Requirements:**
- Supplier database with advanced search
- Performance scoring algorithms
- Document management for certifications
- Communication tracking system
- Contract lifecycle management
- Automated audit reminders

**Estimated Development Time:** 60 hours
**Priority:** High

## Feature: Inventory Control System
**Description:** Real-time inventory tracking and optimization.

**Requirements:**
- Real-time inventory levels
- Location tracking (warehouse/bin locations)
- Lot/serial number tracking
- Inventory valuation
- Stock level alerts (minimum/maximum)
- Inventory cycle counting
- Integration with barcode scanners
- Automated inventory reports

**Technical Requirements:**
- RFID/barcode integration
- Warehouse management system
- Automated data collection
- Mobile inventory app
- Integration with ERP systems
- Real-time synchronization

**Estimated Development Time:** 80 hours
**Priority:** High
