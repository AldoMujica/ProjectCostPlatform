# Supplier and Procurement Features

## Feature: Purchase Order Management
**Description:** Complete purchase order lifecycle from creation to payment.

**Requirements:**
- Automated PO generation from requisitions
- Multi-supplier quote comparison
- PO approval workflows
- PO tracking and status updates
- Delivery schedule management
- Quality inspection integration
- Invoice matching and approval
- Payment processing integration

**Technical Requirements:**
- Automated PO numbering system
- Integration with supplier portals
- Three-way matching (PO/Receipt/Invoice)
- Electronic signature capabilities
- Integration with accounting systems
- Automated approval routing

**Estimated Development Time:** 80 hours
**Priority:** High

## Feature: Invoice Processing System
**Description:** Automated invoice processing with CFDI compliance for Mexico.

**Requirements:**
- XML CFDI invoice import and validation
- Automatic data extraction from CFDI
- Invoice approval workflows
- Tax calculation and validation
- Payment scheduling
- Vendor invoice portal
- Audit trail and compliance reporting
- Integration with SAT (Mexican tax authority)

**Technical Requirements:**
- XML parsing and validation engine
- CFDI schema compliance checking
- Digital signature verification
- Integration with banking systems
- Automated tax calculations
- Compliance reporting tools

**Estimated Development Time:** 70 hours
**Priority:** High

## Feature: Supplier Performance Analytics
**Description:** Comprehensive supplier evaluation and performance tracking.

**Requirements:**
- On-time delivery metrics
- Quality performance tracking
- Cost variance analysis
- Supplier scorecards
- Performance improvement plans
- Supplier certification tracking
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Supplier development programs

**Technical Requirements:**
- Performance scoring algorithms
- Automated data collection
- Dashboard visualization
- Alert system for performance issues
- Integration with quality systems
- Predictive analytics for supplier risk

**Estimated Development Time:** 50 hours
**Priority:** Medium
