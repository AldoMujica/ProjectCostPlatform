# Sales and Quoting Features

## Feature: Quote Management System
**Description:** Complete quoting workflow from client inquiry to order approval.

**Requirements:**
- Create quotes with multiple line items
- Quote versioning and revision tracking
- Client information management
- Automatic quote numbering (COT-AL-XXXX)
- Multi-currency support (MXN/USD)
- Tax calculation (IVA 16%)
- Quote validity periods
- Email quote delivery to clients
- Quote status tracking (Draft, Sent, Approved, Rejected, Expired)

**Technical Requirements:**
- Dynamic quote form with add/remove line items
- Currency conversion with real-time rates
- PDF generation for professional quotes
- Email integration (SMTP)
- Quote templates for different industries
- Integration with CRM system

**Estimated Development Time:** 60 hours
**Priority:** High

## Feature: Sales Control Dashboard
**Description:** Comprehensive sales tracking and performance analytics.

**Requirements:**

- Sales team performance metrics

- Integration with accounting system
- Commission calculation and tracking

**Technical Requirements:**
- Advanced reporting engine
- Data visualization dashboard
- Export to Excel/PDF
- Automated report scheduling
- Integration with QuickBooks/Xero
- Commission calculation rules engine

**Estimated Development Time:** 50 hours
**Priority:** High

**Description:** Client database and relationship tracking system.

**Requirements:**

- Communication history tracking

- Client classification (A/B/C accounts)
- Automated follow-up reminders

- Referral tracking and rewards

- Email integration for communication tracking
- Survey system integration
- Data import/export functionality

**Priority:** Medium
